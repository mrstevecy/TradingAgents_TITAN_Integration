## Titan Integration

This wrapper enhances and extends the original TradingAgents framework with
research-governance extensions aligned with Titan OS 2.9 and Titan DTP 1.6,
including evidence staging, source audit metadata, persistent evidence-retrieval
discipline, user evidence ingestion, technical feature extraction, evidence
graph generation, evidence delta comparison, horizon validation, and
evidence-gated final reporting.

The Titan OS 2.9 and Titan DTP 1.6 corpora, private research materials,
Graphify outputs, generated packets, user inputs, and provider caches are not
included in this public wrapper repository.
